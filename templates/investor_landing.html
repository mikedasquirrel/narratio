risk management

position sizing
1-2% of bankroll per bet using Kelly Criterion with fractional multiplier (0.25-0.5x). Maximum single bet: 2% regardless of confidence. Scales proportionally with bankroll.
confidence threshold
≥65% threshold balances quality with volume (85 bets/season, 69.4% win rate, 32.5% ROI). Lower thresholds (≥60%) increase volume to 406 bets but reduce ROI to 26.5%.
stop loss protocol
Pause betting if bankroll drops >20% from peak. Reassess models and market conditions. Resume only after validation of continued edge. Protects against unexpected market efficiency shifts.
temporal validation
Holdout test spans complete 2024-25 season (2,779 games). Training data pre-September 2024 (4,966 games). No look-ahead bias. Models never see test data during training.
