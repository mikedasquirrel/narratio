domain variables

for entire genus/domain (all organisms of this type)

Д
The Bridge (THE MAGICAL VARIABLE)
Definition
Narrative agency - how much narrative influences outcomes in this domain. Tests if "better stories win".
Type
Scalar ∈ [0, 1]. Д/п > 0.5 passes efficiency threshold.
Д = п × |r| × κ

п = narrativity (openness, 0-1)
|r| = absolute correlation (impact strength, 0-1)
κ = coupling (narrator-narrated, 0-1)
sign(r) = direction (+helps, -indicates role)

Efficiency: Д/п > 0.5 validates narrative agency
Why Absolute Value?
Positive r: Better narratives → better outcomes (narrative helps)
Negative r: Narratives indicate role/context (narrative signals position)
BOTH show narrative matters! |r| captures impact strength regardless of direction.
Testing Protocol
1. Measure domain π (narrativity) from structural characteristics
2. Extract ж (genome) from all organisms
3. Compute ю (quality) using π-guided weights
4. Record ❊ (outcomes) empirically
5. Calculate correlation r between ю and ❊
6. Test efficiency: Д/п > 0.5?
7. Check sign(r) for mechanism understanding

key insights

Absolute Value (|r|)

Narrative potential = impact strength, not direction:

  • |r| = 0.8: Narrative matters 80%
  • sign(r) = +: Better narratives help
  • sign(r) = -: Better narratives indicate underdogs
  • Both valuable! Predictive either direction

Data-First Discovery

Let data reveal patterns, don't predict:

  • Measure exhaustively (all subdivisions)
  • Rank by empirical |r|
  • Top contexts = strongest (from data)
  • Theory explains afterwards
  • Avoids confirmation bias

Taxonomic Optimization

Generic formula finds 20%, optimization finds 50-70%:

  • Decompose by genre, stage, scale
  • Early-stage contexts show higher κ
  • Concept-phase contexts show highest κ
  • Context-specific > generic

Interaction Effects

Combinations stronger than single dimensions:

  • Interaction A×B > Individual A or B alone
  • Context×Feature > Context or Feature independently
  • Test combinations, not just individual factors

Infinite Decomposition

Every organism contains narratives within narratives:

• Organism = Complete pitch/description
• Organs = Product narrative, team narrative, market narrative
• Tissues = Paragraphs, thematic sections
• Cells = Sentences with local п and ж
• Molecules = Phrases with nominative properties
• Atoms = Words with phonetic/semantic gravity

At each level: extract ж, compute ю, test local Д. These aggregate up to organism-level measures.

philosophical implications

The Narrativity Spectrum

Domains exist on a continuum from pure physics (π ≈ 0) to pure perception (π ≈ 1). At the lower end, physical laws dominate and narrative has minimal agency. At the upper end, narrative constructs reality itself.

Most domains fall somewhere in between, where both narrative and external constraints operate simultaneously. The challenge is measuring their relative strength.

Determinism vs Free Will

The framework doesn't resolve the philosophical debate but operationalizes it. Three forces compete: ة (nominative/narrative pull), θ (awareness resistance), and λ (fundamental constraints).

When λ is high (physics dominates), determinism wins. When θ is high (consciousness resists), free will emerges. When ة dominates (narrative constructs), we get a third path: determined by story, not physics.
