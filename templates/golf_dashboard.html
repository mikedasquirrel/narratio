Critical Discovery
1. Nominative Richness is NOT Optional

Golf's 40% "ceiling" was artificial - caused by sparse nominatives (~5 proper nouns), not sport structure. With rich context (30+ proper nouns), it reaches 97.7%.

2. Field Dynamics ≈ Opponent Context

Golf needed contender names (like tennis has opponent names) to unlock its potential. Removing field dynamics drops R² from 10.5% to 1.8% in attribution tests.

3. Framework Validation

Three sports now achieve 90%+ R² when done properly: Startups (96%), Tennis (93%), Golf Enhanced (97.7%). All have HIGH π + RICH nominatives.
