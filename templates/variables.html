how variables relate

Flow: ж (genome) → ю (quality) → ❊ (outcome)

Main Formula: Δ = π × |r| × κ
Threshold: Δ/π > 0.5 means narrative matters

Three Forces (Alternative View):
Δ = ة - θ - λ (regular domains)
Δ = ة + θ - λ (prestige domains)

Gravitational System:
ф + ة create gravitational field
Instances cluster by similarity
Ξ represents theoretical perfection

Validated on 8 domains (Nov 2025):
NHL, NFL, NBA, Golf, Movies, Hurricanes, Supreme Court, IMDB

⚠️ Narrative ≠ Text Story

"Narrative" (ж genome) means ALL information about an instance - structured data, relationships, temporal context, nominative features. Text descriptions are just ONE part of the genome, not the whole narrative.

See: docs/NARRATIVE_DEFINITION.md for full explanation.
