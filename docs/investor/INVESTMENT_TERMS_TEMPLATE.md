## Example Term Sheet

**Amount**: $1,000,000  
**Structure**: 70/30 profit split (Investor/Operator) after 10% hurdle rate  
**Management Fee**: 1% annually ($10,000/year)  
**Performance Fee**: 20% of profits above 10% hurdle  
**Term**: 3 years, with 2-year extension options  
**Reporting**: Weekly summary, monthly detailed, quarterly strategic  
**Governance**: Investor approval for >$5M bankroll or new sports  
**Exit**: Annual redemption windows with 90-day notice  

**Example Year 1 (Conservative Scenario)**:
- Starting Bankroll: $1,000,000
- Ending Bankroll: $1,339,300
- Profit: $339,300
- Less hurdle (10%): -$100,000
- Profit above hurdle: $239,300
- Investor share (70%): $167,510
- Performance fee (20% of profits): -$47,860
- Net to investor: $119,650 (12% return on capital)
- Plus bankroll appreciation: $339,300
- **Total investor return**: $339,300 (34% return)
